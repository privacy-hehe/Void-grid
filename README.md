# VOID-GRID
A lightweight, zero-dependency HTML5 Canvas survival game featuring grid-warping mechanics and momentum-based physics.

##  How to Play
 * **Move:** Click and drag (or touch and swipe) anywhere on the screen to warp the neon mesh. The gravitational pull will slingshot your player orb.
 * **Goal:** Guide the player into the pulsing dashed circle on the right side of the screen to advance to the next sector.
 * **Survive:** Avoid the transparent pink barriers. Touching one instantly resets the current sector.
   😁  ITS EASY
   
##  Features
 * **Custom Physics:** Tuned momentum friction and elastic grid-pull mechanics.
 * **Balanced Difficulty:** Procedurally generated obstacles that scale in count and movement speed, mathematically capped to ensure gaps are always passable.
 * **Persistent Saves:** Your sector progress is automatically saved to your browser's localStorage.
 * **Mobile Ready:** Fully responsive design with built-in protections against accidental iOS swipe-to-refresh gestures.
